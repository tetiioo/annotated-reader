Comment on any document — privately

Open a Markdown file, highlight what matters, and leave comments anchored to the exact words. Here’s the 30-second tour.

📂
1 · Open a Markdown file
It splits into chapters automatically from your # and ## headings, listed in the sidebar.
🖍️
2 · Select text to highlight or comment
Drag to select any sentence: pick a highlight color, or hit 💬 Comment to attach a note.
💬
3 · Click a highlight to read it
Open, edit, or delete any comment. The Comments tab lists them all — click one to jump to it.
🔒
4 · Hand it back privately
Download .md with comments tucks your notes invisibly inside the file. Send it back — the other person opens it here and sees everything.
🔒 Your document never leaves your device
Files are read inside your browser only — there’s no server and no upload. Want proof? Open your browser’s Network tab while opening a file (you’ll see zero requests), or turn off Wi-Fi after the page loads — everything still works.
